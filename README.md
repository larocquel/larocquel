## Who Am I?

Every time I face this question, I find it genuinely challenging to compress a person into a set of words.

Since childhood, I've been fascinated by the universe and by humanity's remarkable ability to start from basic principles and arrive at technologies that once seemed like magic. That sense of wonder never left me, it just found an engineering role to live in.

I want to build things that matter. I want to be at the frontier of human technological progress, to understand how the world's most complex systems work, and to live a life worth telling stories about.
